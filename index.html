About Us

Building Excellence Since 1995

Anoop Singh B.E. (Civil)

Founder of Anoop Singh & Associates with over 28 years of professional experience.

Anoop Singh has performed significant roles as a valuer, design consultant, construction contract administrator, and more on a diversity of project types including government projects. His expertise spans across residential, commercial, industrial, and institutional developments.

He received his Bachelor's degree in Engineering (Civil) from MBM Engineering College, affiliated with the University of Jodhpur, Rajasthan.

Anoop Singh is a corporate member and Chartered Engineer with the Institute of Engineers (India), Kolkata, and an approved valuer with the Institute of Valuers, Delhi. He is also registered as a valuer under section 34AB of the Wealth Tax Act 1957.

He is capable of providing complete structural and architectural design for large projects, working with developers, architects, engineers, or contractors, and offering project management services, architectural design, and expanded services.
